How does it work?

(Hover over each image for more info!)

All you need to do is simply, download our app, register your details and select which medication you would like to order!

1. Order your medication

We now place the order with your GP and our system looks out for your prescription to be transferred electronically to us.

2. We liase with your GP

Our pharmacists will now clinically check your prescription, assembling it ready for an accuracy check by our clinicians.

3. We assemble your medication

Your prescription is then posted to you to be delivered first class via Royal Mail. This is a tracked free of charge service!
